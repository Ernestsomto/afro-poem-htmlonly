Keorapetse Kgositsile

Keorapetse Kgositsiles

19 September 1938 - 3 January 2018

Poet | journalist

Nationality - South-African

Keorapetse William Kgositsile OIS, also known by his pen name Bra Willie, was a South African Tswana poet, journalist and political activist.

Night - 1976: 119

Your hand is heavy, Night, upon my brow.
I bear no heart mercuric like the clouds, to dare.
Exacerbation from your subtle plough.
Woman as a clam, on the sea's cresent.
