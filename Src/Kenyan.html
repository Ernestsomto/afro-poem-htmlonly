Khadambi Asalache

Khadambi Asalache

1935 - 2006

Poet | Author

Nationality - Kenyan

Khadambi Asalache was a Kenyan poet and author who settled in London, England.

Night - 1976: 119

Your hand is heavy, Night, upon my brow.
I bear no heart mercuric like the clouds, to dare.
Exacerbation from your subtle plough.
Woman as a clam, on the sea's cresent.
