Tsegaye Gabre-Medhin

Tsegaye Gabre-Medhin

17 August 1936 – 25 February 2006

Poet | Novelist

Nationality - Ethiopian

Tsegaye Gabre-Medhin was born in Bodaa village, near Ambo, some 120 km from the capital Addis Ababa.

Nile - August 1997

I am the first Earth Mother of all fertility.
I am the Source, I am the Nile, I am the African, I am the beginning!
O Arabia, how could you so conveniently have forgotten,
while your breath still hangs upon the threads of my springs?

Night - 1976: 119

Your hand is heavy, Night, upon my brow.
I bear no heart mercuric like the clouds, to dare.
Exacerbation from your subtle plough.
Woman as a clam, on the sea's cresent.
